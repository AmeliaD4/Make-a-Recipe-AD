Cake mix cookies Recipe

image from something swanky.com

Ingredients

  • 1 box Cake Mix Of Choice (yellow, White, Chocolate)
  • ⅓ cups Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 cups Chocolate Chips, or anything else that works.

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Then mix cake mix, oil and eggs together, blending till well blended(will be thick). Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet. Bake 8 – 10 minutes. Cool and enjoy.
